## 4. Billing and Payment

- All plans are billed monthly unless annual billing is selected
- Payment methods: credit card, ACH bank transfer (Enterprise only), wire transfer (Enterprise only)
- Invoices are issued on the 1st of each month and due within **30 days** (Net 30)
- Failed payments trigger a 7-day grace period before service suspension
- Unused queries **do not** roll over to the next billing period

## 5. Plan Changes

- **Upgrades** take effect immediately; the prorated difference is charged to the current billing cycle
- **Downgrades** take effect at the start of the next billing cycle
- Enterprise contracts require 90 days' written notice for non-renewal

## 6. Free Tier

A limited free tier is available for evaluation and development:
- 500 queries/month

- 1 user
- Community support only (GitHub Discussions)
- No SLA
